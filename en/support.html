How do I delete my account?
Settings → Privacy → Delete account. If you own a home with other members, you must first transfer ownership or remove them.
How do I cancel my Pro subscription?
Subscriptions are managed from your Apple account: iPhone Settings → your name → Subscriptions, or from Settings → Pro → Cancel subscription within the app.
How do I export my data?
Settings → Privacy → Export my data. We'll send you a file with all your information by email.
