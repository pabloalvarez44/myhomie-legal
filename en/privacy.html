3. Legal bases for processing (GDPR)

  • Contract performance — data needed to provide you the service (Art. 6.1.b GDPR).
  • Legitimate interest — anonymous usage analysis and error detection to improve the App (Art. 6.1.f GDPR).
  • Consent — push notifications (you can revoke this at any time from your device settings).

5. International transfers

Some providers (Sentry, Resend) process data in the United States. These transfers are covered by appropriate GDPR safeguards (standard contractual clauses or equivalent privacy frameworks).

6. Data retention

  • Your account and home data are kept while your account remains active.
  • If you delete your account, your personal data is deleted within a maximum of 30 days.
  • Error logs (Sentry) are kept for 90 days.
  • Aggregated analytics data is kept for 14 months (Google Analytics default setting).

7. Your rights

If you reside in the European Economic Area, you have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you.
  • Rectify inaccurate data.
  • Delete your account and associated data.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing.
  • Port your data in a structured format.
  • Withdraw consent at any time (without affecting prior processing).

Exercise your rights by emailing myhomie.support@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days at most. You can also file a complaint with the Spanish Data Protection Agency (aepd.es).

8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data: encrypted communications (HTTPS/TLS), secure authentication via Firebase, and access restricted through Firestore security rules. However, no system is infallible.

9. Minors

MyHomie is not directed at minors under 16. If you become aware that a minor has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Policy at any time. We will notify you of significant changes via an in-App notification. The "Last updated" date at the top of this document reflects the current version.

11. Contact

For any privacy questions: myhomie.support@gmail.com
